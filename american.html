American Girl:

American Girl, a company that makes dolls with the idea of inspiring children to pursue their dreams and educating them on historical events such as WWII, the Civil War, and the American Revolution. Many of these stories are riddled with tragedy, including that of 9-year-old Addy Walker, a black girl who escaped from slavery with her mother and works to help bring her family together. Recently, there has been a call to reflect positive stories of people of color, highlighted by Claudie Wells of the Harlem Renaissance era.
