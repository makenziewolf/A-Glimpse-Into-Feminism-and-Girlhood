What is feminism?

Feminism, according to Forbes writer Kathy Caprino, is the advocacy for political, financial, social and societal equality between the male and female sexes through the campaign for the rights of women. This does not mean degrading men or lessening their rights while uplifting women or female supremacy, the concept that all women are better than all men in every capacity. It is simply allowing women to be viewed and treated the same as men while acknowledging and celebrating their differences.
