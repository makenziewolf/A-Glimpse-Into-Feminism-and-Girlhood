Intersectional Feminism:

Feminism cannot be viewed through just one lens, though. It coincides with all parts of a person’s identity, including race, religion, socioeconomic status, etc. White feminism (feminism for only white women) is still a widely prevalent idea and persistent in the media today, however not overtly expressed. Many see Taylor Swift, the widely popular and successful music artist, as a symbol of white feminism due to her light activism being perceived as performative. As of recently, she donates to women’s charities in most of the cities she performs in.
